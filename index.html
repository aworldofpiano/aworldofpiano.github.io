A World of Piano

Where to play the piano around the world ?

This website aims to reference free-access pianos around the world. Most of them are located in railway stations, malls, but you can find some in unexpected places : parks, universities, or just on the street ! By geolocating them, you can help piano players or anyone interested in hearing music find them and enjoy !
